My Childood

I was born and brought up in a very adorable family. I have grown up with my elder brother and my cousin whom i used to play a lot. I remembered each and everytime we used to play together. We loved to play buwan-buwan, slipper games, tago-tagoan, tumbang preso, and so on. I don't like playing some barbie dolls or wearing girly dresses. I loved watching movies like horror movies, action movies and I really loved to watch Jackie Chan's movies.

My Teenage Life

My teenage life is like being on a roller coaster. It's incredibly active, cheerful, and happening. On the other side, it is also incredibly demanding and confusing. I'm no longer a child or an adult. They refer to us as teenagers. My school is where I spend a lot of time having fun with my pals. I think my pals can understand me better now than my parents can. My parents want me to act like a grownup since they claim I am no longer a child. However, they themselves act as though I'm a child and give me advice on everything I do. I want them to fully trust me, but I need some space.

My Adulthood

My Journey to Adulthood For my whole life, I have had a single thought that sometimes bothers me and causes me to worry about what kind of person I might turn out to be. Even when I was young, I frequently pondered my future plans. I started to experience a lot of stress due to my parents' ongoing demands that I start working at an early age and maintain my outstanding grades. Through it all, I understood that my enjoyment of spending time with my friends and spending the entire day playing video games on my newest console would change.
